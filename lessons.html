Riding Lessons

Become a pro rider!

At Painted Bar Stables, we offer personalized riding lessons for anyone of any age and experience levels. Whether you are a newbie or experienced rider, our trained horses and skilled instructors are here to help you achieve your goals.

What We Teach

  • Riding Safely and Staying In Control in the Arena or Trails
  • Understanding Horse Behavior and Horsemanship Skills
  • Rider Confidence and Independence
  • Grooming, Leading, and Caring For Horses

Lesson Types & Pricing
