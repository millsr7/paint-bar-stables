Trail Rides

Explore nature on horseback

Take in the scenic views on our guided horseback rides through open fields, forest, and hills of New York's Finger Lakes Region. Our trail rides are open year-round and welcome all riders of any experience level. Whether you are a beginner or experienced, our gentle horses and knowledgable guides will make your riding experience memorable.

Trail Ride Options - Ages 10+

  • Scenic Trail Ride 1 Hour | 2 Miles | Walk-Only
  • Spirited Trail Ride 1 Hour | 2.5 Miles | Walk + Trot
  • Adventure Trail Ride 1.5 Hours | 3.5 Miles | Walk + Trot

Booking & Requirements

  • Trail rides are by appointment only
  • All riders must be 10+ years old & be on time of their ride
  • Weight limit: 250lbs
  • Long pants & closed-toe shoes must be worn
  • Riders must give a 36 hours notice to reschedule their ride
