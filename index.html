Welcome to Painted Bar Stables!

Located in the Finger Lakes region, Painted Bar Stables is a community-oriented riding center stable offering trail rides, riding lessons, and camps for kids and adults year-round. With beautiful scenic trails and a welcoming community, we invite you to come along and explore the outdoors on horseback, creating an unforgettable memory.

Why Choose Us?

At Painted Bar Stables, we sincerely believe riding should be safe, fun, and welcoming for everyone of all ages and skill levels. Our trained horses, beautiful trails, and flexible riding options, offer a friendly experience that's unforgettable.
